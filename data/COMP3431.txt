COMP3431 Robotic Software Architecture  
An introduction to Intelligent agent design. Picking actions using planning, learning or
engineered control. Both practical and theoretical components. Practical component: Re-implement parts of
a real agent architecture on a robot. Assignment based. Emphasis on engineering
a working system. Theoretical component: Introduction to a variety of research agent
architectures including classical planning and reinforcement learning. Lecture and lab based.
