COMP1921 Computing 1B  
Covers the topics of COMP1917 not covered in COMP1911. Including: approaches to
problem solving, abstraction, low level structure of memory, memory allocation, stack frames,
secure coding practice, recursion, linked lists.Introduction to ADTs. Introduction to simple binary
trees. Also reinforces concepts introduced in COMP1911: style, unti testing, team programming,
groupwork.
